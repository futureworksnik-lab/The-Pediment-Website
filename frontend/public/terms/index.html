Eligibility & admission

The Leadership Prologue 2026 is open by application. Submitting an application does not guarantee admission — every application is reviewed personally, and selection is at the organiser's discretion. This edition is focused on India's emerging leaders and professionals based in India.

Pricing

Premium Observer seats are offered at Inaugural Early Access — ₹5,000. This is a limited allocation; rates may rise for later allocations. Selected VIP Delegates are onboarded directly, with details shared privately on confirmation.

Payment details are shared with confirmed guests. All sales are governed by the Refund & Cancellation Policy — the Inaugural Early Access fee is non-refundable.
