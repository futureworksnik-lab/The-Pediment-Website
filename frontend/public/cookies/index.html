What we use

This site uses a small number of cookies and similar technologies in two categories:

  • Essential. A single preference stored in your browser that remembers whether you accepted or declined analytics. It contains no personal information.
  • Analytics. Privacy-focused analytics (Plausible) that help us understand how the site is used — page views and how far visitors read. These scripts load only after you choose Accept in the cookie banner. If you decline, no analytics run.

Changing your choice

Your choice is stored in your browser. To change it, clear this site's data in your browser settings and the banner will appear again on your next visit.
