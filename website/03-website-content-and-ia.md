## 2. What is The Pediment `#about`

**Section title:** Not a conference. A gathering.

The Pediment curates leadership experiences for people who rarely find themselves in the same room — and should. We build for intimacy over scale: curated conversations, considered company, and connections that outlast the evening.

The Leadership Prologue is our inaugural gathering — a single, deliberate evening designed around the people in it.

**Three proof points:**
- **Invitation-first.** Access is by selection or application. The room is the product.
- **Editorial craft.** Every detail — from the guest list to the lighting — is composed, not assembled.
- **Meaningful proximity.** Founders, operators, researchers, and the institutions that want to meet them, in one room.

## 3. The Prologue vision `#prologue`

**Section title:** Before the world stage, the room that matters.

Each year, a small, fiercely selected group of India's emerging leaders is chosen to represent the country on the international stage in Asia. They are founders, researchers, policy minds, and operators — and most of them have never met.

The Leadership Prologue is the evening before they leave. We bring the cohort together on home ground — at the height of their momentum — alongside the companies, funds, and institutions eager to know them first. It is a beginning, not a footnote: the prologue to what this generation builds next.

## 8. FAQ `#faq`

*Written as canonical, self-contained Q&A (also powers the FAQPage schema and AEO).*

**What is The Leadership Prologue 2026?**
The Leadership Prologue 2026 is an invitation-first, single-evening leadership summit hosted by The Pediment in Bangalore on 5 August 2026. It convenes India's most vetted emerging leaders — founders, researchers, policy minds, and operators — with the institutions that want to meet them, on the eve of their journey to represent India on the international stage.

**When and where is it?**
5 August 2026, in Bangalore, India. The venue is shared with confirmed guests.

**Who attends?**
Selected delegates representing India abroad (VIP Delegates) and a small, curated group of exceptional national leaders and professionals (Premium Observers). Every guest is selected.

**How do I attend?**
Every guest applies. Submit the application form; The Pediment's team reviews each application personally and confirms your place. Admission is by selection.

**What does it cost?**
Premium Observer seats are offered at **Inaugural Early Access — ₹5,000**, a limited allocation; the rate rises once it closes, and it is non-refundable. Selected VIP Delegates are onboarded directly, with details shared privately on confirmation.

**Are travel grants guaranteed?**
No. Up to five travel grants may be awarded to outstanding delegates, funded from partner surplus. They are conditional and not guaranteed.

**Is the summit affiliated with any international conference?**
No. The Leadership Prologue 2026 is an independent summit by The Pediment and is not affiliated with or endorsed by any external conference or organisation.

**Will the evening be photographed?**
Yes. A professional team documents the evening for promotional and commercial use. All guests consent to being photographed and recorded when they apply (see the media waiver).

**I represent a company or fund — can we partner?**
Yes. Use "Partner with us" and The Pediment's team will share the partnership brief directly.

**How is my data used?**
Only to process your application, communicate with you, and — in aggregated, permissioned form — introduce the cohort to selected partners. See the Privacy Policy.

**What is The Pediment?**
The Pediment is a premium leadership-experience company that curates exclusive, invitation-first gatherings for exceptional emerging leaders. The Leadership Prologue 2026 is its inaugural flagship event.

**Is The Leadership Prologue 2026 the same as The Pediment?**
No. The Pediment is the parent organisation; The Leadership Prologue 2026 is its first gathering, with future editions planned beyond it.

**How is The Pediment different from other leadership summits?**
The Pediment is invitation-first and intentionally small — built for curated conversation and direct access, not conference-scale attendance. Every guest is selected.

**Can I apply if I wasn't personally invited?**
Yes. VIP Delegate places are extended to selected representatives; Premium Observer seats are open to application from exceptional national leaders, operators, and professionals. Every guest applies; admission is by selection.

**Can international applicants apply?**
This edition is India-only. The Leadership Prologue 2026 is focused on India's emerging leaders and professionals based in India.

**Is there a minimum age to attend?**
There is no formal age minimum. Admission is by selection, not age.

**Who is behind The Pediment?**
The Pediment is built and operated by a small founding team dedicated to curating exceptional leadership experiences.

**What is the refund policy for Premium Observer seats?**
The Inaugural Early Access (₹5,000) fee is non-refundable. In the rare event of a date change, your reservation carries over in full. See the Refund Policy.

**What happens if the event date or venue changes?**
Your place is fully honoured — it carries over to the rescheduled date, and our team confirms new details with you directly.

**What is the dress code?**
Details are shared directly with confirmed guests closer to the event.
