# Legal, Entity & Compliance

> Part of **The Pediment — Master Execution Manual** · Section 4. Standalone module.

**Owner:** Founder · **Depends on:** §1 · **Feeds:** §5, §7, §11

> **Not legal/tax advice.** Confirm all figures and flows with a chartered accountant before signing any sponsor. The structure below is the operating intent, not a professional opinion.

### Why a business current account is mandatory

Corporate finance teams will not transfer sponsorship into an individual's personal savings account — they require a **Current Account under a registered business name** to pass internal audit, process invoices, and handle **TDS**. Brand sponsorship / event promotion falls under **SAC 998397 at 18% GST**; sponsoring an unregistered individual triggers **Reverse Charge Mechanism (RCM)** liability for the sponsor. So an invoicing entity is non-optional.

### Primary vehicle — Option B: Partner Entity (this week)

- A trusted contact's **active business entity with a current account** issues commercial invoices to sponsors for **"Event Activation & Brand Promotion."**
- Partner collects funds, accounts for 18% GST, and passes revenue to the founder via a **consulting / sub-contracting agreement**.
- **Why primary:** lets invoices go out to sponsors *immediately*, with GST/TDS handled by an established entity.
- **Guardrail:** if the partner's **educational-business classification** creates a SAC/business-object mismatch on compliance review, switch to Option A without delay.

### Parallel track — Option A: Sole Proprietorship (operational autonomy)

- Register using existing **PAN + Aadhaar** → free **Udyam (MSME) certificate** + local **Shop & Establishment license**, obtainable online in ~**48–72 hours**.
- Banks accept these two proofs to open a dedicated **Business Current Account**.
- Run this in parallel so the venture can transition off the partner entity for long-term control.

### Conditional travel-grant structure

All public- and delegate-facing copy frames the top-5 grants as: **"funded from sponsorship surplus — up to 5 grants."** Never a fixed guarantee. This protects the founder from personal liability if the raise underperforms (§11).

### Media waiver (mandatory checkbox on all registration)

> "I consent to being photographed and recorded for promotional and commercial use by The Pediment and its official partners. I understand these assets may be distributed globally."

### 🔻 MVE Kill-Switch (legal)

None — legal is load-bearing. The invoicing entity and waiver are required at every tier.
