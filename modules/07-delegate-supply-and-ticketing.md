# Delegate Supply & Ticketing

> Part of **The Pediment — Master Execution Manual** · Section 7. Standalone module.

**Owner:** Manager · **Depends on:** §4 · **Feeds:** §3, §9

### Supply pool

Primary target: the **286-member Indian HPAIR delegate cohort** (WhatsApp group access confirmed). These are the VIP supply that makes the demand side pay.

### Two-tier Velvet Rope

| Tier | Who | Price | Mechanism |
|---|---|---|---|
| **VIP Delegate** (supply) | Accepted HPAIR delegates | **₹2,000 refundable deposit** | Attendance **verified at check-in; deposit refunded digitally within 48h post-event** (no cash handling at the door — protects the luxury, frictionless aesthetic). Filters casual sign-ups, secures high-fidelity attendance data, targets a 95%+ show rate for sponsors. |
| **Premium Observer** (demand) | Curated national emerging leaders / students / emerging professionals | **₹5,000** | Prices out casual attendees; curates an ambitious room; ~₹2.5L immediate revenue at 50 seats. |

### Seat allocation (286 pool → ~100 VIP seats)

Curated + first-committed hybrid: deposit-paid delegates secure seats in order, subject to a light quality/relevance screen. Publish the cap to create scarcity. Waitlist overflow.

### Application & screening flow

Website form (§8) captures **Name, LinkedIn URL, University, domain/sector, Tier (VIP vs Observer)** + mandatory media-waiver checkbox → deposit/payment → onboarding into the WhatsApp UGC engine (§9). This same data compiles into the **Delegate Dossier** that Talent Pod / Title sales depend on (§5) — so registration must open in W1.

### Backlash mitigation

Charging delegates ₹2,000 — even refundable — while the founder profits, and without HPAIR's name, carries reputational risk (§11). Mitigate by (a) keeping it explicitly **refundable (verified at check-in, paid back digitally within 48h)**, (b) leading value messaging with *recruiter access + conditional travel grants + premium experience*, (c) never overclaiming HPAIR affiliation.

### 🔻 MVE Kill-Switch (ticketing)

If deposit collection/refund ops overwhelm the lean team, switch VIP to a **free RSVP with a signed commitment** (no money movement) and lean harder on Observer tickets for cash.
